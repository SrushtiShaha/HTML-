Seating Arrangements :

Cafes looking to maximise their seating capacity need a strategy that strikes a balance between customer comfort and increasing profit. With more customers seated throughout the day you can enjoy a higher turnover of tables for every service, supported by an appropriate layout that adds to the design.The comfort aspect is directly related to the amount of space you have and ensuring you do not overcrowd. Of course, the furniture needs to be comfortable, but comfort also means giving customers enough of their own personal space to relax and enjoy the service. Also think about how the seating arrangements affect the interior design and how they can complement each other.

Always Clean the Table :

The Dining room is the heart of any restaurant and the first area visitors interact with. Strict cleanliness and hygiene protocols must be followed in this area. This not only ensures the safety of the customers but helps build trust with the people dining at the restaurant.It is not just the food and hospitality that attracts a customer to a hotel or restaurant space. Moreover, the customer is drawn more to the environment, ambience, decor and overall cleanliness and hygiene the restaurant space offers.Finally, training your housekeeping staff will not only give them a sense of understanding on how to organise and structure to perfection but will train them to do it quickly and efficiently.
